About my Drone

I have an HS110D drone made by Holy Stone. I have had this drone since I was only in 5th grade, but after doing a few test runs, it was clear that I should probably wait another year or so. I didn't end up taking out that drone until 8th grade 2023. The reason I remembered about it was because we had a school PBL (Project Based Learning), and it was about aircraft, specifically focusing on helicopters, planes, and drones. Anyway, I started to get a little excited, and so one day after school, I went with my little brother to go take it for a fly...
